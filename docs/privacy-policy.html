Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Companion for Cacao" (the "App") handles your information. The App is developed by isdabenx (dev.isdabenx) as a companion tool for the Cacao board game.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. You are not required to create an account or provide any personal details to use the App. The App does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other sensitive permissions on your device.

Data Storage

All data generated or used within the App, such as game scores, settings, and preferences, is stored locally on your device using an SQLite database. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted over the internet or shared with any third parties. You can delete all data associated with the App at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, including analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. There are no external dependencies that collect or process your data.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for users aged 3 and older (PEGI 3 / ESRB Everyone). Since the App does not collect any personal information from any user, it is fully compliant with privacy regulations regarding children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
